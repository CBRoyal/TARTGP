What You Need to Pack for The Amazing Race

Like any other sort of vacation, you're going to need to pack some necessary items that you need for your trip around the globe. It's always important to pack and bring necessary equipment while on the go and this webpage will show you all the necessary items that you need to pack and bring with you for The Amazing Race.

Necessary Items

Here are the necessary items that you definitely need to bring for your race around the world:

  • Proper clothing (shirts, pants, socks, shoes, hats, bathing suits for anything involving swimming, different layers of coats for colder temperature environments, buffs, etc.)

  • Travel-size hygiene products

  • Medical kit containing bandages, eyepatches, prescribed medications, over-the-counter-products, medicine, etc.

  • Plenty of water to stay hydrated.

Items That You Can't Bring With You

Here are some items that you're not allow to bring with you for The Amazing Race:

  • Your own money & credit card(The Amazing Race crew will provide you with leg money for certain transportation purposes and a credit card to pay for the flights you book.)

  • Travel guides

  • Translation books

  • Electronics including cell phones, smartwatches, cameras, nor GPS (Teams are allowed to borrow phones from locals however or just ask for help.)

Items that You Should Bring With You Just in Case

Here are some items that you should bring with you for your trip around the world just in case:

  • Clicker or calculator

  • Flashlights

  • Language Guides

  • Journals to document everything you did and happened during your Amazing Race season including where you went, what you did, the elimination order of other teams, the positions that you've finished in each leg of the race game twists encountered, country flags, currencies of the countries you visited, what you saw, etc.

  • Compass

  • Contact lens for people with vision problems
